ICONIC PORSCHE'S!

The Porsche 911 GT3 RS
is a high-performance sports car that truly embodies Porsche’s commitment to pushing the boundaries of what is possible. It features a 4.0-liter flat-six engine, a sharp chassis, and a wild aero design. The GT3 RS delivers unbelievable performance on the racetrack, with up to 518 hp, it can take you from 0-60 mph in 3.0 seconds, and can reach a top track speed of 184 mph.


Learn more

The Porsche 918 Spyder is a mid-engined, plug-in hybrid supercar built from 2013-2015. It was powered by a naturally-aspirated 4.6-liter V8 engine that was capable of producing 875hp. The Spyder was a technology masterpiece that pointed to a bright future for sports car fans. With its impressive power, it could accelerate from 0-60 mph in just 2.6 seconds.


Learn more

The iconic Porsche 917 is most famous in its 917K form in Gulf colors. The Porsche 917 was developed into multiple variants over the years, pushing the boundaries of knowledge in aerodynamics. The beautifully proportioned Langheck and the experimental low-drag Pink Pig 917/20 are notable examples. The ultimate 917, however, would have to be the mega-powerful 917/30 that proved so successful in the American Championships they were termed “Can-Am killers”.
